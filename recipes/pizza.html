New York Style Pizza

This pizza is almost identical to the famous slices sold on the streets of New York. And so much better than delivery!

Ingredients

  • High gluten flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Put water in mixing bowl.
  2. Add salt and sugar.
  3. Add flour and yeast
  4. Add oil
  5. Mix everything until smooth
