Flour Tortillas

Homemade flour tortillas

These tortillas are cheap and easy to make. They make a great companion to any meal.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Combine flour salt and baking powder
  2. Add oil and water
  3. Mix
  4. Knead
  5. Rest dough
