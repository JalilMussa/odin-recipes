Grandma Lasagna

This homemade lasagna is like the italians make it. Cheap, flavourfull and for the heart.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Cheeses
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
